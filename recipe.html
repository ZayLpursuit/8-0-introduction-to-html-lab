Honey Garlic Salmon


Description

This recipe teaches you how to make a tasty Honey Garlic Salmon.


Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup of Honey
  • 1/4 cup of minced Garlic
  • Salmon filet
  • Butter
  • Parsley
  • 1 Tablespoon of salt
  • 1 Tablespoon of Black pepper

Directions

  1. Heat pan and add oil
  2. Season Salmon(garlic, salt, pepper)
  3. Add butter and honey to pan
  4. Place Salmon in mix
  5. Add Parlsey and Enjoy!
